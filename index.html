Nutrition Facts

Here’s a simple guide to help you build a balanced meal.

Protein

Helps keep you full and supports your body.

  • Eggs
  • Chicken
  • Beans
  • Yogurt
  • Cheese
  • Tuna

Carbohydrates

Give you energy for the day.

  • Bread
  • Rice
  • Pasta
  • Oats
  • Potatoes

Healthy Fats

Support the brain and help meals feel satisfying.

  • Avocado
  • Olive oil
  • Nuts
  • Peanut butter
  • Seeds

Fruits & Veggies

Add fiber, color, vitamins, and crunch.

  • Spinach
  • Tomatoes
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Apples
  • Bananas

Easy meal formula

Try to build a meal with: protein + carb + fruit/veggie + optional fat.

Helpful Tips

  • Don’t worry if you’re missing ingredients — recipes can be flexible.
  • Start with simple foods like eggs, rice, pasta, bread, or cheese.
  • Use the open link if you want full directions from the source.
  • Little wins count. Cooking something from your fridge is awesome.
  • Try mixing one protein, one carb, and one veggie for a balanced meal.
